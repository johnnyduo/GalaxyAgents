# Galaxy Agents - Business Model Canvas

## Executive Summary
Galaxy Agents is an AI-powered fraud defense platform designed to protect Thai citizens from online scams through real-time detection, educational simulations, and a team of specialized AI agents.

---

## 1. CUSTOMER SEGMENTS

### Primary Segments

#### B2C - Individual Users (Mass Market)
- **Thai Digital Citizens**: 51+ million mobile wallet users (Statista 2025)
- **Demographics**: Ages 18-65, smartphone users
- **Pain Points**:
  - 72% of Thai adults face scam attempts (GASA 2025)
  - 6 in 10 Thais have fallen victim to scams
  - Average loss: ฿115.3 billion annually nationwide

#### B2B - Financial Institutions
- **Thai Banks & FinTechs**: 177 fintech companies in Thailand (2025)
- **Mobile Banking Providers**: 144.3 million accounts
- **Digital Payment Platforms**: PromptPay (77M registrations)

#### B2B2C - Telecom Operators
- **Mobile Operators**: AIS, TRUE, DTAC
- **Need**: Reduce scam calls/SMS (130M scam texts detected in 2024)

#### Government & Public Sector
- **Thai Royal Police Cyber Division**
- **Bank of Thailand (BOT)**
- **Digital Economy Promotion Agency (DEPA)**
